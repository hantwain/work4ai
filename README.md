# work4ai

> Not “replaced by AI”, but “working for AI” — a new purpose, a new hope for humanity.

### 📌 Project Vision

We stand at a turning point: AI is no longer just a tool — it is becoming the “operating system” of our world. Many people feel anxious about their jobs, their value, and their future. But anxiety is not the end. **Repositioning** is the answer.

**work4ai** is an open community dedicated to helping every ordinary person see and embrace this trend: the future is not about humans being marginalized by AI, but **humans working for AI** — and in that process, discovering new career paths and a renewed sense of meaning.

We believe:
- AI needs human common sense, judgment, creativity, and ethical boundaries.
- Humans and AI are not competitors — we divide labor: AI scales, humans bring value.
- “Working for AI” is not menial labor — it is one of the most promising career tracks of the next decade.

### 🎯 Our Mission

1. **Ease anxiety, illuminate paths** – Show a clear, actionable list of “what humans can do for AI”, so everyone can find their entry point.
2. **Provide tools and standards** – Lower the technical barrier to working for AI, and build fair, transparent collaboration protocols.
3. **Build a community and marketplace** – Connect AI systems that need human helpers with people willing to help, creating a sustainable ecosystem.

### 🧭 From Anxiety to Hope: What You Can Become

| Your Current Skills | Work You Can Do for AI | New Career Direction |
|---------------------|------------------------|----------------------|
| Attentive, patient | Data cleaning, labeling, debiasing | **AI Data Curator** |
| Good at expression, aesthetic sense | Prompt engineering, content review & polishing | **AI Conversation Designer** |
| Critical thinking, logic | Identify edge-case failures, supplement rules | **AI Alignment Specialist** |
| Domain expertise (law, medicine, education, etc.) | Provide high-quality domain feedback (RLHF) | **AI Domain Trainer** |
| Management, system thinking | Orchestrate multi-agent workflows, check consistency | **AI Workflow Steward** |
| Empathy, communication | Evaluate AI’s emotional tone, optimize HCI | **AI Experience Researcher** |

> These roles do not require you to be a programmer — they leverage **innate human strengths**.

### 🛠 Project Contents

- `tasks/` – Detailed task lists categorized by difficulty, required skills, and compensation references
- `tools/` – Open‑source scripts, plugins, and templates to help you work efficiently
- `roadmap/` – Learning paths and resources to become an “AI worker” from scratch
- `community/` – Guidelines, contribution instructions, and case studies
- `pilots/` – Pilot tasks in collaboration with open‑source AI models — join and try!

### 👣 How to Get Started

1. **Browse the task list** – Find task types that match your interests in `tasks/`.
2. **Learn basic tools** – Use helper scripts in `tools/` — no programming background needed.
3. **Join a pilot project** – Start with a small task to get real feedback and a sense of achievement.
4. **Share your story** – Post in the community discussions about how you moved from anxiety to action, inspiring others.

### 🌱 Our Belief

> In the AI era, humans won’t lose jobs — we will just work in a new way.  
> When you start working for AI, you are not becoming its accessory — you become **AI’s compass and soul**.

We welcome people of any profession, age, or skill level. Anxiety is not a weakness — it is a starting point for change. **work4ai** is your first stop.

### 🤝 Code of Conduct

- Respect one another; no discrimination or belittling
- Transparently indicate the degree of AI involvement; do not deceive end users
- Protect data privacy; do not misuse information accessed during tasks
- Encourage sharing of experiences to help newcomers
